*Multi-regional freight transportation platform*

This User Agreement (hereinafter — the **“Agreement”**) governs the terms of use of the **tmGo** platform (hereinafter — the **“Platform”**) provided by the platform administration (hereinafter — the **“Administration”**). By registering on the Platform, you unconditionally accept the terms of this Agreement.

The Platform is designed to facilitate interaction between parties who need freight transportation and parties who provide transport services. The Platform is not a carrier and is not responsible for the actual performance of the carriage contract.

## 1. Terms and definitions

The following terms are used in this Agreement:

### Platform

The **tmGo** software suite, available via the website and mobile applications, that enables posting freight requests, finding carriers, communication between parties, and transaction support.

### User

Any natural or legal person registered on the Platform and having accepted the terms of this Agreement.

### Customer (Cargo owner)

A User who posts a freight request specifying route, cargo characteristics, timelines, and conditions.

### Carrier

A User (natural or legal person) providing freight transportation services based on Customer requests. The Carrier bears full responsibility for having the licences, permits, and insurance required by the laws of the jurisdiction where they operate.

### Dispatcher

A User acting on behalf of a Customer or Carrier under a power of attorney or employment contract. The Dispatcher is responsible for actions taken on behalf of the represented party.

### Request

A Customer’s offer posted on the Platform containing carriage conditions; acceptance by a Carrier creates contractual relations between them.

### Deal

A freight carriage contract concluded between a Customer and a Carrier through the Platform. **tmGo** is not a party to that contract.

### Account

The User’s personal account containing identification data, activity history, and rating.

### Rating

An aggregated reputation score for a User based on counterparty feedback and transaction history on the Platform.

## 2. Subject matter of the Agreement

**2.1.** The Administration grants the User a non-exclusive right to use the Platform in accordance with its functionality, including:

- posting and searching freight requests;
- viewing carrier profiles and ratings;
- communicating via built-in chat;
- tracking shipment status;
- leaving reviews and ratings.

**2.2.** The Administration may amend the Agreement by notifying Users through publication of a new version on the Platform. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

**2.3.** The Platform is provided “as is”. The Administration does not guarantee uninterrupted operation and is not liable for losses due to temporary unavailability of the Platform.

## 3. Registration and account

**3.1.** Registration is required to use Platform features. Users must provide accurate and up-to-date information.

**3.2.** Users are solely responsible for safeguarding credentials (login, password) and for all actions performed under their account.

**3.3.** The Administration may request supporting documents to verify identity or authority. Failure to provide them or provision of false information may result in account restriction or blocking.

**3.4.** A person may have no more than one active account. Duplicate accounts violate this Agreement.

**3.5.** Users may delete their account via a request through the Platform. Completed transaction history may be retained by the Administration in accordance with applicable record-keeping laws.

## 4. Rights and obligations

### 4.1. User obligations

Users undertake to:

- provide accurate information in registration and listings;
- hold required permits and licences for their activities (for Carriers);
- not post knowingly false requests or offers;
- not use the Platform for fraud, misleading counterparties, or other unlawful purposes;
- not complete deals off-Platform to evade fees unless otherwise provided by the pricing plan;
- communicate respectfully; threats, insults, and discrimination are prohibited;
- promptly notify the Administration of unauthorised account access.

### 4.2. User rights

Users may:

- use Platform features available under their plan;
- leave reviews about counterparties after a deal is completed;
- contact Administration support on disputed matters;
- delete their account as provided in clause 3.5.

### 4.3. Administration rights and obligations

**The Administration shall:**

- maintain technical availability of the Platform;
- notify Users of changes to the Agreement;
- receive and review User inquiries within reasonable time;
- protect personal data in accordance with the Privacy Policy.

**The Administration may:**

- suspend or block accounts for violations;
- remove listings or content that breach Platform rules;
- change fees with at least 7 calendar days’ prior notice;
- disclose User data to competent authorities on lawful request.

## 5. Requests, responses, and deals

**5.1.** A Customer’s request is a public offer. The Customer’s confirmation of a Carrier’s response constitutes a carriage contract directly between them.

**5.2.** The Platform is not a party to the carriage contract and is not liable for performance, including cargo safety, deadlines, or service quality.

**5.3.** The Carrier confirms they hold valid permits required for the specific shipment, including cross-border permits where applicable.

**5.4.** Cancellation of a confirmed deal is governed by cancellation rules published on the Platform. Repeated unjustified cancellations may lower ratings or lead to blocking.

**5.5.** Parties prepare transport and accompanying documents (waybills, CMR, etc.) themselves. The Platform may provide templates for information purposes only.

## 6. Ratings and reviews

**6.1.** After each deal, both parties may leave mutual reviews. Reviews must be objective and based on actual interaction.

**6.2.** False, abusive, or defamatory reviews are prohibited. The Administration may remove violating reviews and take action against offenders.

**6.3.** Ratings are aggregated indicators and should not be the sole basis for choosing a counterparty. Users are solely responsible for selecting counterparties.

## 7. Liability and limitations

- losses from improper performance of a carriage contract;
- accuracy of information User profiles and requests;
- Users’ unlawful conduct;
- losses due to technical failures, force majeure, or third-party actions.

**7.2.** The Administration’s aggregate liability to a User shall not exceed the amount of fees that User actually paid in the 3 (three) months preceding the claim.

**7.3.** Users are liable for losses caused to the Administration or third parties by breach of this Agreement.

**8.2.** By registering, Users consent to processing personal data to the extent necessary for the Platform to function.

**8.3.** Users may withdraw consent by emailing the Administration. Withdrawal prevents further use of the Platform.
